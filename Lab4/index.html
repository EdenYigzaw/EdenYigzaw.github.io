Paws & Pets Veterinary Clinic

We are a small clinic located in Toronto Ontario that are dedicated to providing the best care to our customers. Our team of consists of committed professionals who understand your love for your pet. At Paws & Pets Veterinary Clinic most of us have pets at home, so we know exactly how important your pet is to you! We are passionate about building relationships with every four-legged family member who walks through our doors and ensuring they stay happy and healthy.
